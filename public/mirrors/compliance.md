# PharmaSaha - Compliance & Verification

The uncompromised standard for pharmaceutical integrity in West Africa. 

PharmaSaha operates a strictly enforced zero-tolerance framework against counterfeit and sub-standard formulations. Our compliance matrix serves as the ultimate barrier between our sovereign partners and the global unstructured supply chain.

## 1. Source Verification
We enforce absolute integrity from origin. EU-GMP and WHO-PQ dossier audits occur before cargo ever leaves the manufacturing facility in Europe or Asia.

## 2. Cold Chain Integrity
Environmental parameters are monitored in real-time. We guarantee -20°C to +8°C absolute continuity across air-freight and overland final-mile delivery.

## 3. Sovereign Customs Clearance
Utilizing ECOWAS fast-track clearance methodologies alongside NAFDAC and FDA liaison protocols, we eliminate standard bureaucratic delays at the port level.
